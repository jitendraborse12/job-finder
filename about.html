Why choose us

JobHunt was found in 2016 on repeated requests from our existing and potential customers who were looking for Simple, Scalable, Agile, Robust, Hire to Retire modules with in-depth functionalities. They were also looking for the above solutions a valuable price.

Firstly, we have built a solution that provides data based on ‘facts’ instead of mere assumptions. Secondly, our software embraces the DIY approach, similar to a factory, which is highly configurable, robust, scalable, and the implementation is just a matter of a few hours. And thus “Jobhunt”..
